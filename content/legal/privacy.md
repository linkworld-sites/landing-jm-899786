# Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how JM ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website.

## Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name and email address when you submit a commission inquiry.

We also collect certain information automatically when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, and pages visited. This information is used to improve our website and understand how visitors use it.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:
- Respond to your commission inquiries
- Process and fulfill orders
- Communicate with you about your orders
- Improve our website and services

## Analytics

We use analytics tools to understand website traffic and user behavior. This helps us improve the site experience.

## Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, please reach out through our commission inquiry form on the website.
