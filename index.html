How It Works

From Idea to
Wearable Masterpiece

A simple, collaborative process designed to bring your vision to life with zero guesswork and total creative freedom.

01 💬

Share Your Vision

Tell us your idea — a theme, an image, a feeling. Send references, mood boards, or just a few words. We'll shape it into a concept.

02 🎨

Sketch & Approve

We draft a detailed sketch for your approval before a single stroke of paint touches fabric. Revisions included — no surprises.

03 ✋

Hand Painted

Using professional-grade fabric acrylics, we paint your design by hand. Each layer is sealed for wash-resistant, long-lasting colour.

04 📦

Delivered to You

Your jacket arrives carefully packaged with care instructions. Worldwide shipping, tracked and insured every step of the way.

05 🌟

Wear It Forever

Your one-of-a-kind piece is built to last. Colours stay vibrant wash after wash — a wearable artwork for years to come.
